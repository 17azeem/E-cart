Product Name: BioBag Compostable Trash Bags

Details: These bags are made from a bio-resin derived from starches of plants and vegetable oils, and printed with soy-based inks. They are certified home compostable by TUV Austria, BPI Certified, and CMA Certified, and will break down in 10-45 days in a compost environment.

Uses: These bags are a great replacement for plastic trash bags, and can be used for food waste, yard waste, and other compostable materials. They are available in various sizes, including smaller produce bags and handled shopping bags.

Replacement: These compostable trash bags are replacing traditional plastic trash bags, which can take hundreds of years to decompose in a landfill and contribute to plastic pollution in the environment. By using compostable bags, you can help reduce waste and support sustainable practices.
